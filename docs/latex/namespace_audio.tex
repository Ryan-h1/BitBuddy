\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Provides functionalities for audio playback and control within the application. 

The \doxylink{namespace_audio}{Audio} namespace encapsulates methods for playing sound effects, initializing the audio system, muting/unmuting sound effects, and adjusting the overall volume. It\textquotesingle{}s designed to offer a simple interface for managing sound effects within the application, leveraging Qt\textquotesingle{}s multimedia capabilities.
