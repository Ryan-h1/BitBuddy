\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \doxylink{class_music_service}{Music\+Service} is responsible for playing and stopping the music in the game. 

\begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
Ryan Hecht
\end{DoxyAuthor}
This service is a singleton that is responsible for playing and stopping the music in the game. It uses a QMedia\+Player in a loop to play music. It can only play one track in its current rendition.
